Colorful Evolution
About Methods Data Results Credits

How has the use of color in the mainline Pokémon games evolved over time?

The ultimate goal of my project is to analyze the use of color in video games over time through the Pokémon game franchise. Through this analysis, I hope to be able to evaluate and visualize how the color in video games has changed over time and also find the patterns within these changes.


- Are games getting more colorful?
- How has the evolution of gaming hardware impacted game design and experiences?
